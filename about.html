WHO AM I?

a versatile mind

THE PERSON

I was born in 1996, and spent all of my life in Michigan. Growing up, I developed an interest in a variety of creative hobbies, including building with LEGO and KNEX, playing and writing music, video games, and drawing, the latter of which I started pursuing at the age of 10 and would keep as an active hobby going into my college years.

THE STUDENT

My education and interest in design started in 2015, when I became a student at Hillsdale College. Through their Graphic Design minor program, I learned the basic elements and principles of design. Wanting to take my studies in the field farther, I transferred to Oakland University in 2018 and graduated Magna Cum Laude with a B.A. degree in Graphic Design in 2020.

THE DESIGNER

Freelance experimentation with design and illustration has given me a wide set of skills that incorporate all sorts of media, including logo and brand design, marketing campaign design, print media design, motion graphics, photo/video editing, traditonal and digital illustration, and game design. As a person of many interests, this list is sure to only expand as my career develops.
